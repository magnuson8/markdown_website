Student Testimonial

I am a past student of yours from both CYC 423 and CYC 424 courses and will be graduating in November.

Just a little update if you are interested;

To say I was challenged in these courses would be an understatement. I remember thinking I am never going to get a CYC job where these courses would be useful.. this is so hard!.why must they torture us like this, how dare they stretch our CYC brains and make us cry!?!?!??!?!

Well. to my surprise, I have just completed my first month as an Analyst in the Quality Assurance division with MCFD. If it werent for these two courses, I would have zero chance at getting this job. Since being in this position, I have had to recall on many of the skills I learned in your courses and have also introduced some of these concepts to my coworkers.

Ive realized I am actually quite interested in data analysis and am starting to think about perusing further education with qualitative and quantitative data analysis processes.

So basically, what I am getting at here is Thank-You. I didnt understand why we were being taught this at the time (which I am sure comes up a lot for students and I am sure you hear a lot of whining about), but it has made an impact on what direction I would like to take for future education and a career. I am excited about whats to come!

Doug Magnuson
